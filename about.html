SALON MIRROR





Salon Mirror is located at at the Winner's entrance at the Quinte Mall (South Side, Bell Blvd. parking lot).

In 2005, Salon Mirror & Day Spa opened in the Quinte Mall, Belleville as an uptown sister to The Salon & Day Spa located in Downtown Belleville. The goal was to provide extended convenience to clients and bring a new level of hair salon and day spa to a mall environment. The mall location provides extended hours seven days a week, convenient parking and accessibility to all clients.

Within one year of opening Salon Mirror & Day Spa had to expand it's space to accommodate the volume of traffic and it now boasts over 3000 square feet of stylish salon and spa space and only 2 years of that expansion, Salon Mirror is now undergoing a major renovation to serve YOU better.

Offering full salon and day spa services, Salon Mirror and Day Spa is the most stylish and convenient salon & spas in the region.
